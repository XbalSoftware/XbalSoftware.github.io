EYEbrary

A patient education and exam summary generator for eye care professionals.

About EYEbrary

EYEbrary is an iPad app designed to help eye care professionals create clear, patient-friendly written summaries following an eye examination.

Clinicians can select starter templates, edit the text freely, and generate polished PDF reports for patients. The app is intended as a documentation and patient-education tool. It does not provide automated diagnosis or treatment recommendations.

All clinical information included in generated reports is selected, reviewed, and edited by the clinician using the app.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the EYEbrary application (“the App”) handles information.

Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information with the developer.

All information entered into the App remains on the user’s device unless the user chooses to export or share a generated document.

Patient Information

Users may optionally enter patient names or other clinical information while preparing patient summaries or reports. This information is used only on the device to create documents. The developer does not receive, access, or store this information.

Data Storage

Information entered into the App is processed locally on the device. The App is not intended to function as a persistent patient database. Once the clinician clears the form, patient-identifying information entered for that report is not retained by the App’s working form or history view.

Data Transmission

The App does not transmit information to the developer or to external servers. If the user chooses to share or save a generated PDF, that action occurs through services controlled by the user’s device, such as Mail, printing, AirDrop, Files, or other sharing options.

Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use analytics services, advertising frameworks, tracking technologies, or third-party data collection.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party services for the purpose of collecting or processing personal information.

Security

Because information handled by the App remains on the user’s device, data security depends in part on the security of that device and the user’s chosen sharing methods. Users are responsible for protecting their devices and exported documents in accordance with applicable privacy obligations.

Professional Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring that their use of the App complies with applicable privacy laws, professional standards, and regulatory requirements in their jurisdiction.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date shown above.
